ABOUT US

Pizza Pizzeria is the best pizza spot in Nairobi.

We have been in business for over 20 years and we offer a variety of different pizza flavours, made in traditional italian fashion, using recipes that have been well refined over time.

Our pizzas are made using organic, locally-sourced fresh ingredients, thus the assured quality of our pizzas.

All of our pizzas are cooked using our custom-made wood-ired oven, designed and manufactured in Italy. This gives our pizzas an edge over the other pizzas.
